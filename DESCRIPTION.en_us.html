Inspired by lettering seen on the posters Oscar M. Bryn made for the Santa Fe Railroad, Calistoga is a cheerful display face whose style that remains the flavor of fancy yet comfortable Railroads trips and the West of the United States.

Calistoga is a semi condensed saving space display typeface of a strong and kind personality displayed also in its wide sets of figures that range from proportional linning to tabular Old Style, as well as a variety of fractions.

Its vintage Railroad flavor goes until its symbols, currency, and punctuation which also includes also case sensitive forms.

Calistoga is an original typeface designed by Yvonne Schuttler. Eben Sorkin expanded the language support and refined the design in 2018.

Calistoga is published by Sorkin Type.
